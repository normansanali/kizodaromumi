    In a perfect lesson on high-low dressing, Kendall Jenner paired two everyday staples with a casual-yet-luxe piece for an easy, chic day look.

    Yesterday, the supermodel stepped out in New York City to promote her 818 Tequila in a breezy end-of-summer outfit. It consisted of a classic white tank top from Hanes, a silky white midi skirt with a delicate textured floral print by Dries Van Noten, and glossy black leather loafers by the Row. (Shop similar styles below.) Jenner finished the elegant ensemble with a black leather tote bag by the Row, skinny black rectangle sunglasses, and silver drop earrings. She wore her straight hair down and had on natural makeup.

    Jenner first stopped at the Sugar Factory in Times Square, then popped over to Park Avenue Liquor Shop, a rep for her tequila brand told Bazaar.com.

    Jenner never misses with her effortlessly sophisticated model-off-duty style. Earlier this week, she stepped out in the perfect no-fuss going-out top for a date night with Bad Bunny. She wore the silky cream-colored button-down with sleek straight-leg pants cinched at the waist with a black leather belt featuring a golden buckle, and finished the minimalist look with glossy black pointed-toe heels, a slouchy black leather shoulder bag, and small hoop earrings.

    And earlier in the summer, the model also did high-low dressing for a coffee run in Los Angeles. She wore a long-sleeved black scoop-neck sweater and mid-rise black straight-leg pants, which she cinched at the waist with a black leather belt with a silver buckle. Jenner finished the laid-back ensemble with black leather flip-flops by the Row, a tan tote bag, black rectangular sunglasses, and tiny gold hoop earrings.
