    On February 27, 1993, Alphonse Francis Areola was born in France. He currently plays goal for Premier League team West Ham United and the France national team.

    After graduating from the PSG academy, he spent time on loan with Lens, Bastia, and Villarreal to start his professional career. While playing for Villarreal, Areola set a club record for the longest period of time without conceding a goal, going 620 minutes.

    He returned to PSG in 2016, and he finally made over 100 appearances there. Areola made a season-long loan to Real Madrid in 2019.
