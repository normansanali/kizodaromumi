    About Fabolous Net Worth

    Fabolous is a rapper from America. He grew up in Brooklyn and became known while he was still in high school. He got his first big break when he performed live on DJ Clue’s radio show and later on Hot 97. DJ Clue then signed him to his Desert Storm record label, and Fabolous also secured a distribution deal with Elektra, a major music label. Fabolous rose to fame with his debut album called “Ghetto Fabolous,” which had popular songs like, Can’t Deny It and Young’n.

    Fabolous Biography

    John David Jackson, who is widely known as Fabolous, was born on November 18, 1977, in Brooklyn, New York City. He comes from a mixed heritage, with Dominican and African-American roots. Fabolous grew up in the Breevort Houses located in the Bedford-Stuyvesant neighborhood of Brooklyn. During his senior year of high school, he started rapping under the name Fabolous Sport, which was a reference to the Polo Sport line by the fashion brand Ralph Lauren. Eventually, he shortened his stage name to just Fabolous. Interestingly, the misspelling of the word “Fabulous” as “Fabolous” was accidental. While freestyling, he mistakenly spelled it that way, and the unconventional spelling ended up sticking with him.
