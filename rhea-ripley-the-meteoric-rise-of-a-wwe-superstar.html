    16 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Since entering the world of professional wrestling, it is safe to say that Rhea Ripley's journey has been nothing short of special.

    Between her NXT debut to her current place as one of the more feared female wrestlers, Ripley's road to the top has been highlighted by numerous significant events and altering moments that have helped define her wrestling persona.

    Rhea Ripley in WWE has shot up the wrestling popularity in skyrocketing fashion and in this article, we look into a detailed overview of Ripley's rise in WWE. 

    Early Career and NXT Stint

    In 2017 Rhea Ripley arrived at WWE, via the NXT brand, and immediately made an impact.

    She platformed in the debut episode of the NXT UK Women's Championship tournament; where she won to be crowned the first-ever NXT UK Women's Champion.

    On 7 September episode of NXT, Ripley won NXT Women's Championship and became the first NXT title holder to defend a title at a WrestleMania, WWE's flagship event.

    Landmark Achievements in WWE

    There were multiple milestones in Ripley's WWE journey.

    She is also a one-time Raw Women's Champion and a one-time WWE Women's Tag Team Champion, and the first female Australian titleholder in WWE history.

    Winning the 2023 Women's Royal Rumble match as the number one entrant crowned her as a top player in pro wrestling.

    WrestleMania Triumphs

    At WrestleMania 40, Rhea Ripley had her WrestleMania moment against Becky Lynch in a highly anticipated Women's World Championship match.

    Ripley showed her mettle and resiliency in retaining her WWE Women's World Title with her hard-fought win over Lynch.

    WWE Elimination Chamber is Homecoming Success

    What a homecoming this was for Ripley the following night at WWE's Elimination Chamber.

    Her first feel-good match in front of her home continent of Australia was a crowning achievement, as she energized the crowd and won by count out, continuing her impressive undefeated streak since 2022.

    In this homecoming victory, Ripley not only showed her strength in the ring, but she's now captured the hearts of fans around the world.

    Evolution as an artist and content creator on social media

    Ripley's influence goes beyond WWE's weekly TV.

    Her widespread social media demographic and personal engagement with followers on social channels has given her something very few female gamers have, scope to grow and reach new mass audiences globally.

    Her propensity to take hold of opportunities and challenges, combined with her unparalleled mixture of charisma, athleticism, and power have enshrined her as a compelling anomaly in the world of professional wrestling.

    Stealth Character Growth and What This Could Mean Later

    With Rhea Ripley still forming into a performer and learning characters to be more experienced, her development as a character is crucial to her growth.

    With WWE wanting to push her as a brand, strategic alliances must be crafted to keep her relevant on every WWE show beyond simply bolstering her Judgment Day faction.

    WWE must now execute thoughtful character building if it wants to keep the audience interested and keep the mystique of Rhea Ripley while not going overboard with her.

     

    Rhea Ripley's extraordinary journey in WWE is a true reflection of her dedication to the sport, presence in the spotlight and in-ring skill.

    Ripley's blood, sweat and tears - from her formative years in NXT, to her current status as a dominant force in the worldwide wrestling scene - have been a testament to her grit, resolve and determination.

    And as long as she keeps plugging away in WWE, her reach and the mark she leaves on the industry will only grow, further reinforcing her place in the annals of pro wrestling history.
