    Paire Socks: These $19 socks have been dubbed the 'comfiest in the WORLD'

    09 Aug 2024, 00:00

    A pair of socks designed by two best friends have been named the ‘most comfortable in the world’ thanks to their unique makeup in the cut, the cushioning and the cuff.

    Paire was created by Melbourne entrepreneurs Nathan Yun and Rex Zhang because Nathan was sick and tired of finding holes in his wool socks and wanted something that would last.

    He and textiles expert friend Rex set out to make socks from a 50/50 wool and cotton yarn together, and their socks (from $19.90) have since won thousands of fans across Australia and the world.

    A pair of socks designed by two best friends have been named the ‘most comfortable in the world’ thanks to their unique makeup in the cut, the cushioning and the cuff (socks pictured)

    Paire was created by Melbourne entrepreneurs Nathan Yun and Rex Zhang because Nathan was sick and tired of finding holes in his wool socks (Paire socks pictured)

    So what makes Paire socks so special? 

    Firstly, it’s the material. Paire socks are made from a blend of Australian Merino wool and organic combed cotton.

    This unique mix has been designed to be warm in the cold and cool in the summer. 

    It’s also moisture-wicking to repel sweat and odour-absorbing, so you can get more wear from your socks and need to wash them fewer times.

    Paire socks are made from a blend of Australian Merino wool and organic combed cotton that has been designed to keep you cool in summer and warm in the winter (socks pictured)

    Paire socks have also been designed with comfort in mind. 

    They hug the foot at the exact right angle of your foot and heel and stay in place all day, no matter what activity you’re doing. 

    The socks feature invisible arch support and a hand-sewn toe that will minimise blisters, skin irritation and any potential bunching at the toe.

    Finally, the ‘cloud cushioning’ of the fit means that they hug your sole and ankle. 

    The socks are designed for all sorts of wear, whether that is everyday use, commuting or hiking.

    Thousands of shoppers who have tried the socks have left rave reviews on the Paire website, with one woman saying it feels like you’re ‘walking on a cloud’ (pictured)

    Thousands of shoppers who have tried the socks have left rave reviews on the Paire website, with one woman saying it feels like you’re ‘walking on a cloud’ when you wear them.

    ‘These socks are so comfortable and I’m not even joking. Feels like stepping on clouds with the cushion. Incredible value for merino socks too. I’ll definitely come back and get more for the family,’ one reviewer wrote.

    ‘I don’t go anywhere without these socks now. Especially when I work out. They’re comfortable, soft, absorbs sweat perfectly and doesn’t stink. These are my favourite socks now,’ another added.

    A third man wrote: ‘Wife approves. Great socks. The 90 degrees angle hugs your heels and the toes are so comfy with this seamless design. Never thought socks could make so much difference on the feet’.

    There are currently five different colours available in the socks: Snow, Fog, Black, Pinot and Sky, as well as three different styles available (Paire socks pictured)

    There are currently five different colours available in the socks: Snow, Fog, Black, Pinot and Sky, as well as three different styles available.

    You can either buy calf socks (designed to sit from the calf to the toe), ankle socks (sit at the ankle for everyday wear) and active ankle socks (for the sporty type designed to sit at the ankle).

    The socks are available for men and women, and can be machine washed, although air drying is advisable. Prices start from $19.90.
