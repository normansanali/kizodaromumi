    05 Sep 2024, 00:00

    The drama series Locke & Key returned to Netflix for a third season on 10 August 2022 and so did Connor Jessup, to continue to portray his role of Tyler Locke, the Locke family’s eldest son. And so, viewers who are used to seeing him as the son of his onscreen parents on the show, Nina Locke (Darby Satanchfield) and Rendell Locke (Bill Heck), are wondering who real Connor’s parents are.

    Let’s explore in the writing below.

    Who Are Connor Jessup’s Parents?

    Connor Jessup’s parents are Bill Jessup and Brenda Thurston. He was born to them on 23 June 1994 in Toronto, Canada.

    Meet Connor Jessup’s Father, Bill Jessup

    Connor Jessup does not get tired of saying “I love you” to his sweet old man/oldsie on social media.

    And Bill Jessup, the father, also often does the same on his own social media.

    • Bill Jessup Age

    Bill Jessup was born before 1960. So, he should have turned 62 years old in 2022.

    • Bill Jessup Job

    Bill Jessup is a Toronto-based sales representative with Bosley Real Estate Ltd., Brokerage. On his BIO, Bill claims that his more than 30-year career as a professional in financial trading and sales has provided him with a reputation for integrity and a strong appreciation for the importance of client partnerships. At the same time, he asserts that he has been actively involved in the community through volunteering and coaching his son’s hockey teams. (Connor or Liam’s he did not clarify though.)

    • Is Bill Jessup On Instagram?

    Yes. Bill Jessup can be found on Instagram @billejessup and there were 15 posts and 344 followers on it as of 10 August 2022. At the same time, Bill also handled two Facebook accounts, one for his personal endeavors and the other for business purposes.

    Meet Connor Jessup’s Mother, Brenda Thurston

    Connor Jessup and his mother, Brenda Thurston have a very special relationship. Often they mention and gush about one another on their respective social media.

    Connor says things like he is obsessed with his radiant, kind, bizarre, gray-eyed mother who in her younger days resembled a character from a Claire Denis movie. Connor also once said that his mother decided one day to be a mother and wound up unusually brilliant at it.

    As for Brenda, she sees her son as this young man who is non-judgmental, empathetic, and in love with books and film, travel, and romance. Most importantly, the matriarch loves her son’s “gay” more than is possible. So you know, Connor came out publicly as gay in June 2019.

    About his mother, Connor also once shared that she loves movies and has always been very supportive of his desire to act in one.

    Connor may be kenned for his role in Locke & Key, but he has been acting on screen for over a decade.

    • Brenda Thurston Age

    Every every Benda on the 28th of May, Brenda Thurston is just as excited about her birthday. And so in 2022, she was not any less pleased as she turned 62 years old.

    • Brenda Thurston Job

    Brenda Thurston happens to be an ASD consultant and behavior analyst at Surrey Place which is based around Toronto, Ontario Area. If you already don’t know, an ASD consultant is an autism spectrum disorder consultant. So, a great part of Brenda’s job is to assist and address challenging behaviors and teach adaptive skills to children, youth, and adults with ASD and their families.

    Speaking of her education, Brenda got her Honours Science Degree in Animal Sciences from the University of Toronto from 1979 through 1983. Later, it was from OISEOISE that she did her master’s degree in Special Education and Teaching.

    • Is Brenda Thurston On Instagram?

    Yes. Brenda Thurston could be found on Instagram @brendathurston1 and it had 254 posts and 1,236 followers as of 10 August 2022.

    Brenda also shared glimpses of his life on ‘Brenda Thurston’ Facebook and on Twitter @bjessup4. Her BIO on the latter read “mother, friend, fellow citizen…let’s be kind to one another.”

    Related FAQs

    • Are Connor Jessup’s Parents Still Married?

    We hate to break it to you that Connor Jessup’s parents are no longer together or married even. However, they anyway managed to not let their separation affect their friendship and especially their role as parents. They have seemingly moved on with life. Connor’s dad at least clearly has with his partner Colette Currin.

    With Corrin, who originally came from Orkney, South Africa, Bill has been together since at least 2013.

    • How Many Kids Do Connor Jessup’s Parents Have?

    There is one more son in the picture. However, it was not understood if he is Connor’s full sibling or half-sibling. One thing at least is true that he is Bill Jessup’s son. It is Liam Jessup we are talking about. He is into music and could be found on IG @254eljay. Also, according to his Facebook, he studied at Northern Secondary School and lived around Toronto, Ontario.

    Liam turned 24 on 7 May 2021. And on the occasion, Connor took to his IG to call him his “sweet fierce brilliant brother”.

    • Where Do Connor Jessup’s Parents Live?

    Connor Jessup’s parents despite being no longer together lived in the same city. And maybe even the same neighborhood around Toronto, Ontario in Canada.
