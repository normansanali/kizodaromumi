    Alejandro Fernández’s First Son Is Named After Him 

    Alejandro Jr. Fernández was born in Mexico on November 4, 1994, as the first child of Mexican celebrity duo América Guinart and Alejandro Fernández. His mother, América, is an architect from America, while his father is a Mexican pop star.

    Alejandro is presently 29 years old and is the oldest child among the third-generation Fernández children. He is Mexican by birth and is of Hispanic ethnicity. According to his birth date, Alejandro Jr. has a Scorpio sun sign. He doesn’t seem to have acquired many educational qualifications, but that doesn’t mean he isn’t educated.

    He had a high school education, at the very least. There is no record of him attending any tertiary institution, whether in Mexico or outside the country. Thus there isn’t any information about Alejandro’s educational background yet. However, there is plenty to know about his career.

    Apart from the fame, he has as Alejandro Fernández’s first son, Alejandro Jr is also a popular Mexican singer in his own right. At 29, he is a professional singer with two albums under his belt. Considering his family background, this isn’t even a surprise.

    First, he is the son of two famous Mexican singers, and then he is the grandson of Mexican musical icon Vicente Fernández. He was encouraged to become a singer after Vincente overheard him singing one day. He began to compete in multiple musical talent shows to improve his craft. He is also a trained pianist.

    When he decided to go professional, he was managed by none other than his grandfather. Some of his best-known songs include the quintessential love song, ‘Lo Primero Qué Hara’ and ‘Te Amare’ (2018). His first album ‘Sigue la Dinastia’ was released in 2019. He has not yet released a second one since then.

    Alejandro Fernández Jr. is available on multiple social media platforms but is most popular on Instagram, with close to a million followers. On Twitter, he has about 17k followers and over 580k followers on Facebook. The 29-year-old singer is reportedly worth about $34 million as of 2022.

    The bulk of his earnings come from royalties from his music and tours, while the remainder comes from the revenue from his pastry shop, named La Doniteria, located in Zapopan, Mexico. Alejandro Jr. is also a married man. In 2021, he married his long-term girlfriend, Alexia Hernandez, with whom he had been engaged since 2010.

    They tied the knot on May 14, 2021, and they already have one child. On March 17, 2022, the new couple welcomed their first child, Mia Fernández, to the world. Mia is the overall second grandchild of Alejandro Fernández.

    Alejandro Fernández ‘s Second Child is Camila Fernández

    Camila Fernández is the second child and first twin daughter of Alejandro Fernández and América Guinart. She is currently 26 years old and was born on November 30, 1997, in Guadalajara, Mexico. Like her elder brother, Camila is Mexican by nationality and Hispanic by ethnicity. She belongs to the Sagittarius zodiac sign. She has four siblings—two full siblings, one of whom is her twin sister, and two half-siblings.

    Camila Fernández is fully trained in playing multiple musical instruments, including the violin, flute, guitar, ukulele, and percussion drum. She also took ballet classes and theater art classes as a little girl.

    After high school, Camila then proceeded to Berklee College of Music, where she obtained her degree in Music. Camila is a singer and actress and has followed the footpath of most of her family before her. She is currently signed to Universal Music Mexico and has worked as a background singer for her father.

    One of the highlights of Camila’s musical career was when she performed the Latino version of the Disney song, The Circle of Life, alongside her father on the album ‘We Love Disney.’ As an actress, she appeared on Grandes Temas de Telenovela, a television program featuring her father.

    This was her debut TV appearance. Camila is currently married to a man named Francisco Barba. They have a daughter together, and her name is Cayetana. Cayetana was born on Thursday, March 17, 2022, in Guadalajara, Jalisco. She also has a son whose name hasn’t been made public yet. According to multiple sources, Camila’s son was the first grandson of Alejandro Fernández.
