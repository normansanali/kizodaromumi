    Blair O Neal Bio

    Blair O’Neal is a professional leading golf player and a sports model in America. She is best recognized as the winner of Golf Channel. Blair O Neal is also known as a model of various runway fashion shows.

    Age, Appearance, and Measurements

    Blair was born on May 14, 1981, in Macomb, Illinois United States. She belongs to a Native American family. Blair holds an American nationality and her ethnicity is unknown. Blair has a height of 5 feet 5 inches. Her body weighs 60kg.

    She has blonde hair and blue eyes. Talking about her body measurement, her waist size is 25 inches and hip size is 35. Her bra size is 34B. Furthermore, her shoe size is 8(US) and the dress size is 6(US).

    Blair O Neal Early Life

    She is very close to her mother and father and she keeps sharing her pictures with her parents on her social media handle. Her parents shifted to Arizona when she was a child where she spent her early life there. When she was 11, she and her brother joined a local junior golf clinic at San Marcos Golf Resort in Chandler, Arizona. Her father enrolled both of them. At the same time, she practiced at Dobson Ranch Golf Club in Mesa. When she became 13, she started playing for the American Junior Golf Association (AJGA)

    Blair O Neal Education

    She was a good student. Blaire completed her high school education from Corona Del Sol High School in Chandler, Arizona. Later she got a scholarship at Arizona State University.

    Husband

    Blair O’Neal was once rumored of dating the famous singer Justin Bieber, but none of them talked about it. A Few years back, there was a rumor that Blair was having an affair with Bubba Watson, but she didn’t accept it.  There are no rumors of her dating anyone at the current time.

    O Neal Golf Career

    Blair O’Neal began her golf career as a beginner at San Marcos Golf Resort in Chandler, Arizona. By the age of 13, she started playing for the American Junior Golf Association. At that time, she had already proved herself as one of the best young golfers in her state.

    She won the “Women’s 5A Arizona State Golf Championship” at a very young age. She won “NCAA women’s long-drive champion” award twice and Golf MVP title four times. Eventually, Blair was selected for the U.S. Junior Ryder Cup Team in Valderrama, Spain. After that, she was selected for the AJGA Canon Cup Team. Since then, Blair O Neal began playing several international games. She became the runner-up in the Woman’s Western Tournament and she won AJGA Ping Phoenix Junior Championship as a junior national golfer.

    Blair O Neal began her modeling career as a runway model for various fashion shows. In her early modeling days, she was known as a television model. She also appeared as a model in several print ads, catalogs, magazine covers, websites, and commercial advertisements. In 2009, she competed on Golf Channel’s Big Break: Prince Edward Island and finished runner-up. The following year, she earned more fame after winning the reality television program, Big Break: Dominican Republic.

    Later in 2010, she made to the final stage of LPGA Qualifying School and she signed on with Cobra-Puma as her lead sponsor. In 2008, she was named as one of the “Hottest 50 Athletes of All-Time” according to Sports Illustrated and in 2013; Golf Magazine named her the “18th Most Beautiful Women in Golf”. In 2015, she announced her retirement as a full-time golfer.
