    Kamil McFadden Facts

  • He spent his early years in Alabama and Georgia before moving to Los Angeles, California.
  • Kamil McFadden was 6 years old when he started acting.
  • As a voiceover artist, he made his theatrical film debut by providing vocals to the character of Claude in the 2012 3D computer-animated action-fantasy film, Rise of the Guardians, which stars Chris Pine, Alec Baldwin, Jude Law, Isla Fisher, and Hugh Jackman.
  • He made his first theatrical film appearance as Bumpty Fluzoo, the son of Malcolm and Mary who falls for Charlotte, in the 2013 comedy film, Grown Ups 2, which is the sequel to the 2010 film Grown Ups.
  • From 2015 to 2018, Kamil McFadden brought to life the character of Ernie Cooper, K.C. and Judy’s brother, and a stereotypical nerd, in the Disney Channel comedy series called K.C. Undercover. He co-starred in the show alongside Zendaya, Veronica Dunne, Trinitee Stokes, Tammy Townsend, and Kadeem Hardison.
  • Kamil played Ernie Cooper in an episode titled Scary Spirits & Spooky Stories of the Disney Channel comedy series, Austin & Ally, in October 2015.
