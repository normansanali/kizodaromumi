    There's a Sons of Anarchy baby on the way! Maggie Siff is expecting her first child with her husband, the actress confirms to Us Weekly.

    "I haven't really announced [it] yet," the 39-year-old told Us at the Evening With Sons of Anarchy event at the Leonard H. Goldenson Theatre in North Hollywood, Calif., on Friday, Oct. 25. "I'm past the nausea so I'm feeling a lot better, a lot more energetic. We just finished shooting the season, which is good."

    The Bronx native doesn't have a baby bump yet, but her pregnancy hasn't necessarily been a secret. Siff — who's played Tara Knowles on the FX series since 2008 — recently revealed she was expecting via Twitter with the addition of "future mom" posted on her account profile.

    Siff and her husband know what the gender of the baby is, but are keeping it a secret for now. As for pregnancy cravings? "A little bit of ice cream," she said. "Nothing too much. Yoghurt [and] dairy."
