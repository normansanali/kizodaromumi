    Is it possible to give Cheerios to chickens?

    Yes, hens adore Cheerios and other morning cereals, including corn flakes. It is alright to feed them, but be sure to split them up and only give them to them as a special treat every now and again. As a result, there is no doubt that hens can and will consume Cheerios.

    Is it possible for geese to consume bananas?

    Bananas are a regular diet for our geese, and they love them. They like eating the stools; we drop the stool after we have picked the bunch of bananas and split it, and then call the geese, who enjoy eating the interior of the stool/stem after it has been split.

    What is the preferred meal of a goose?

    A medium-sized goose will consume around 200g of food each day if it does not have access to grass. Cauliflower leaves, cabbage leaves, and lettuce are among the greens that geese like. They will also eat leftover vegetables such as boiled potatoes, carrots, parsnips, and other root vegetables, however not all geese are interested in the same foods and must be introduced to new foods gradually.

    Banana peels are safe for hens to consume.

    Yes, banana peels are OK. They can eat the peel, although they don’t do it very often. They will eat them if you use a food processor to ground them down to a fine enough consistency.

    Apples are a favourite of geese, however the Canada Geese found them difficult to consume when afloat in water. Although one of the apples was a hard Bramley cooking apple, which they couldn’t eat because of its hardness, the other, which was a softer eating apple, was a hit.

    Is it bad for geese to eat bread?

    Popcorn, if it has been popped, is entirely okay for birds to consume.
