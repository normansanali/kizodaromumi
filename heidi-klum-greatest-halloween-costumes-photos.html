    There’s no one who loves Halloween quite like Heidi Klum. See her best costumes over the years, right here.

    If there’s one celebrity who is guaranteed to go all-out for Halloween year after year, it’s Heidi Klum! The model has been hosting a star-studded Halloween party since 2000, and she really takes transforming to the next level with her Halloween looks. Whether she’s a Hindu goddess, cartoon character, or something gorier, Heidi’s never shied away from a shocking look.

    Many have dubbed Heidi the “Queen Of Halloween,” and she’s here for it. “I embrace that title!” she said in a 2018 interview. “And I want to live up to it year after year. When I moved to America, I immediately fell in love with Halloween and the spookiness of it. All that was missing was a fantastic party. It felt like a real opportunity for me.”
