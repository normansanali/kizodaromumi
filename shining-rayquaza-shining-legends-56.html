    Origin

    Dragon Pulse is a move in the Pokémon games that Rayquaza can learn. Sky Judgment is an attack that first appeared on Rayquaza from Legends Awakened. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon X. This card features the Shiny version of Rayquaza.
