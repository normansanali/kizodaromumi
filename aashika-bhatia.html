    Wiki/Biography

    Aashika Bhatia was born on 15 December 1999 (age 19 years; as in 2018) in Surat, Gujarat. Her zodiac sign is Sagittarius.

    Family, Caste & Boyfriend

    Aashika Bhatia belongs to a Hindu Family. Her father, Rakesh Bhatia, is a businessman. Her mother, Minu Bhatia, runs a beauty salon in Surat. Her parents have separated their ways and Aashika resides with her mother. She has a younger brother, Dev Bhatia.

    Career

    Aashika Bhatia made her television debut in 2009 with NDTV Imagine’s TV serial “Meera” in which she played the role of the young ‘Meera.’

    Aashika Bhatia as young Meera

    Next, she essayed the role of ‘Ginni’ in the TV serial “Parvarrish – Kuchh Khattee Kuchh Meethi.” She earned wide popularity with her performance in the TV serial.

    Post Parvarrish, Aashika made a stint in Sony TV’s tv serial “Kuch Rang Pyar Ke Aise Bhi.” She portrayed the role of Dev’s sister, ‘Nikki Dixit.’ Bhatia also featured in the TV show Ek Shringar- Swabhiman. She even featured in two episodes of the crime series Gumraah.

    Aashika Bhatia in Kuch Rang Pyaar Ke Aise Bhi

    In 2015, Bhatia made her Bollywood debut with the film “Prem Ratan Dhan Payo” in which she appeared in the role of ‘Rajkumari Radhika,’ Salman Khan’s sister.

    Aashika Bhatia in Prem Ratan Dhan Payo

    In addition to serials and films, she has also featured in various TV commercials including the TVC of “Badshah Masala” and “Clinic Plus.”
