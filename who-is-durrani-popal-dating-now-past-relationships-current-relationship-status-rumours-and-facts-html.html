    Who is Durrani Popal?

    Durrani Popal was born on May 6, 1989, in San Francisco, California, and is of Afghan descent. She first rose to fame as a cast member of the reality TV show “Dash Dolls,” which followed the lives of the employees of the Kardashian-owned store, Dash. She has also appeared on “The Spin Crowd” and “Shahs of Sunset.”
