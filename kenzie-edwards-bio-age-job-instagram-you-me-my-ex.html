    TLC You, Me & My Ex Season 1 came in 2021 and explored several couples. One thing was common in each one of these couples. One partner was reluctant to let go of their ex for one reason or another. After two years, when some of these cast members returned for Season 2, the network also introduced to its viewers two new additions. One of the newbie groups comprises Matt, his girlfriend Kenzie, and his ex-girlfriend Chelsea. So this particular storyline follows the exes as Chelsea moves in with Matt and Kenzie.

    In this writing, we shall tell you who Kenzie Edwards is and what her journey has been like in You, Me & My Ex.

    Kenzie Edwards On You, Me & My Ex

    So, this is how Kenzie Edwards, her boyfriend Matt “Matthew” Borton, and his ex Chelsea’s story played out on the show.

    Chelsea and Matt dated for several years before a “dramatic” breakup broke them apart. And so, a month later, Matt started dating Kenzie. Chelsea was naturally not happy with him moving on so quickly. So, the two decided to remain best and only friends even years later.

    Fans were quick to notice Matt dropping everything to help his ex, even if it meant putting her above his current girlfriend. So, Chelsea finds herself without a place to live after selling her house, Matt allows him to move in with him and Kenzie. Kenzie of course, did not look thrilled about her new roomie.

    Is Kenzie Edwards Still In A Relationship With Matt?

    Indeed. Kenzie Edwards was still together with Matt as of April 2023. There was so much pictorial proof to prove so on Kenzie’s social media. For instance, her private IG account not only did include a picture of her and Matt as its Display picture, but here Kenzie also linked to Matt’s profile, pointing out him as her boyfriend.

    Kenzie’s social media also suggests to us that she and Matt have been together since at least 2019. While the most recent we saw the two on her social media post was on 24 April 2023. They looked really together and in love.

    Kenzie and Matt have not yet been married. However, they sure have been seen dressed up for a friend’s wedding that attended together back in September 2021.

    In her life, Kenzie also is very grateful to be attending to her 2 pets Finely (cat) and Charleigh (dog).

    Matt, on the other hand, has had a job as a sales manager at Hardwood Bargains for the past four years. Before this, he studied Business Administration and Management at Middle Tennessee State University (MTSU).

    Kenzie Edwards Age

    Kenzie Edwards, AKA Mackenzie L Edwards, was born in 1994. So, she turned 28 years old in 2022.

    Kenzie Edwards Job

    Other than being a fur mama and the lover of country music, Kenzie Edwards on her social media profile also admits to being a Kindergarten teacher. She goes on to give details of it in her job profile. Here’s what we know.

    Kenzie has been teaching for more than 5 years and has been teaching at Christiana Elementary School (CES) for all of them. She admits that she spent four years teaching 1st grade and a year teaching Kindergarten. At the time of this writing, she seemed to be in her 2nd year of teaching Kindergarten.

    Speaking of her own schooling, she said she studied Elementary Education at MTSU and attended Rutherford County Schools for K-12.

    If you also need to get a hint at her salary, a report from indeed.com indicates that she should get paid $16.05 per hour on average working in Tennessee.

    Is Kenzie Edwards On IG And Facebook?

    Yes. Kenzie Edwards can be found on IG and Facebook. As of 27 April 2023, her IG @kenzayyyyleigh included 12 posts and 456 followers. Yet, it was kept private.

    On her ‘Kenzie Leigh Edwards’ Facebook though she did not mind showing regular glimpses of her life publicly.

    Kenzie Edwards Family

    Kenzie Edwards’s mother is Crystal L Edwards. She turned 51 years old in December 2022. On her Facebook, she mentioned being a former alum of Glencliff Comp High School as well as a Murfreesboro, Tennessee native and a Nashville, Tennessee-born.

    Then, there is Brian Thomas Edwards, Kenzie’s dad. He reached the age of 51 in 2022. He also marked Murfreesboro, Tennessee as his current residence. Other than that, on LinkedIn, he mentioned being a senior project coordinator at Earl Swensson Associates since March 2006. If you also need to know, he has been working at a company that provides design services of architecture, interior architecture, master planning, and space planning to clients globally in healthcare, hospitality, education, corporate office, and senior living industries as well as community projects and the arts.

    Kenzie’s parents have been still together and married, since tying the knot on 8 April 1994.
