    Who is Enola Bedard?

    A professional dancer, actor, choreographer, TikTok celebrity, and social media influencer from Canada is Enola Bedard. She is well-known for being a contemporary and hip-hop dancer. Her internet fame has been greatly influenced by her dance videos, especially on TikTok and Instagram.

    For well-known companies including Fashion Nova, Pretty Little Thing, and Dolls Kill, Enola Bedard has modeled. She additionally posed with many photographers, such as Estuard Guinea.

    Career

    She works as a model, dancer, actress, TikTok celebrity, and social media influencer. When she was 13 years old, she started taking dancing lessons. She then received a scholarship in Los Angeles, California, to further her dance.

    The TikTok celebrity competed in dance contests for her school dance performances. She has also given performances on numerous occasions, such as the Daddy Yankee concert at the Crypto.com Arena’s Calibash music festival. She also had a performance at the Billboard music festival.

    She has a large following on TikTok because of her dance and choreography videos, where she is well-known. Most of the dancer’s videos are shot in open spaces near the Hollywood Walk of Fame on Hollywood Boulevard. She currently has more than 15 million followers and 310 million likes.

    Her popularity has spread to other social media sites, including Instagram, which as of this writing has 1.8 million followers. The Instagram star posts vacation images and dance videos featuring various dancers on her account.

    The social media star started a YouTube channel with the same name on October 7, 2017. She posts videos of herself dancing, working out, and TikTok-related material. The channel currently has over 2 million subscribers.
