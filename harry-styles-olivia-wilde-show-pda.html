    Harry Styles and Olivia Wilde are continuing to keep cozy.

    The new couple were photographed together again after reports revealed the two colleagues (who are working together on the film Don't Worry Darling) are indeed, dating. The pair's relationship was confirmed after photos of the pair attending the wedding of Styles' longtime manager Jeff Azoff, were released.

    In the new images, Styles sports gray sweatpants which he paired with a brown hued hoodie, a hot pink knit beanie by La Ligne, and white oval framed sunglasses and matching sneakers. Wilde happened to be wearing the same outfit from last weekend when she was first spotted with her new beau, which included an argyle print sweater also by La Ligne, brown pants, and platform canvas sneakers. Both Styles and Wilde, whom were reportedly out with close friends for the day, wore protective face masks as well, due to the alarming rising rates of COVID-19 across the nation, but especially California. More images of the pair showing off a bit of PDA can be seen here.

    According to Entertainment Tonight, the pair have been seeing each other for a few weeks now and their romance blossomed in a "very organic" way.

    "Olivia and Harry are together for long days on set and have gotten to know each other really well, so it's all very organic," a source told ET.

    People also reported that the two spending so much time together on set quickly turned into a blossoming romance.

    "What started out as a close friendship, quickly turned romantic. Everyone saw it coming. During breaks, he's hasn't been able to stay away and visited her trailer," said the source. "[When] they were in Montecito, California this weekend for a wedding, they were affectionate around their friends, held hands and looked very happy."
