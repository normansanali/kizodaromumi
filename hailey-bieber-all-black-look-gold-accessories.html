    Hailey Bieber's latest look is all about the avant-garde accessories.

    The Rhode founder attended the opening party for OBB Studios in Hollywood yesterday, and before heading to the event, she shared a peek at her high-fashion look on an Instagram story. The model turned to Schiaparelli for the outing, pulling a sleek look from the house's spring 2023 collection that featured several of their signature gold charms.

    The outfit was comprised of a long-sleeved bustier jacket, with a wide square neckline and gold front zipper, and matching black cigarette pants. The set was cinched with a wide black belt, featuring an ornate gold buckle and three hanging charms, including a gold seashell and an ear pierced with a pearl adornment. Bieber also paired the look with hanging charm earrings including eye and ear pendants.

    As for her glam, the model wore her dark brown hair in a neat bun and paired her "glazed donut" makeup look with smoky eyeshadow and a rosy pink lip.

    Hailey Bieber//Instagram

    The style star was also spotted out on Friday night, as she attended her celeb pal Lori Harvey's 26th birthday party at The Fleur Room in Hollywood. Bieber chose a strapless minidress by Alexandre Vauthier, which had a sheer lace bodice and wrapped tulle fabric that left peeks of skin around the torso. She styled the piece with sheer black stockings, black satin pointed-toe heels, and chunky gold earrings.

    Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

    The model previously talked about her personal style for Harper's BAZAAR's September 2022 cover story, and shared that she looks to the late Princess Diana for fashion inspiration.

    "I was really inspired by the fact that she was the most-looked-at woman in the world at that time, of all time, and she did what she wanted with her style," she explained. "She really expressed herself through her style despite being in the position she was in."
