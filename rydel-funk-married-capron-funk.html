    Rydel Funk is still married to her husband Capron Funk and the couple welcomed their third child, a boy named Storm Rocky-Ross Funk on September 8, 2023. The child was named after Rydel’s mother Stormie Lynch while his middle name is a merge of her (Rydel’s) brothers Rocky and Ross. 

    Rydel became married to Capron, a YouTuber in September 2020 and welcomed their first child in 2021 and their second in 2022. Her love life seems picture-perfect and her career also keeps putting her in the limelight. She is an American musician, dancer, actress, businesswoman, and founder of the pop-rock band R5 alongside her brothers.

    All to Know About Her Relationship and Love Life with Capron Funk

    The Couple Started Dating in 2019 After She Dated Ellington Ratliff

    Rydel Lynch began dating her husband, Capron Funk in 2019. Capron asked her to be his girlfriend while she was at a concert with her brothers’ band, The Driver Era in Los Angeles in June 2019.

    He asked her in a very unusual manner. The YouTuber held (with the help of others) a sign showing “Will you be my girlfriend?”. It turned out that Rydel was interested and she shared images of the process on her Instagram page, calling Capron “a keeper”.

    In 2020, she became engaged to her now-husband Capron. The same year, they got married at Secret Garden, Phoenix, Arizona.

    Rydel’s husband is a YouTuber and a scooter rider. He is also a part of the Funk Bros with his brother Corey Funk. He started the joined YouTube channel in 2015 and started a personal change in 2019.

    Away from her husband, she dated fellow R5 member Ellington Ratliff. They dated from 2015 to 2018.

    Rydel Has Welcomed 3 Children in 3 Years with Her Husband 

    Since the couple became married in 2020, they have welcomed three children in 2021, 2022, and 2023. The first child is a boy named Super Funk. He was born on April 12, 2021.

    The second child is a girl named Sweetie Funk. She was born on August 2022.

    The couple again welcomed another child, a son named Storm Rocky-Ross Funk on September 2023. Rydel and her husband made the announcement of the child on their Instagram pages on 11 September 2023.

    Other Facts About Rydel Funk You Should Know About Her

    She Was Born Into a Close Knitted Music Family

    The entertainer cum businesswoman was born Rydel Mary Lynch on August 9, 1993, in Littleton, Colorado. She is the second and only daughter among 5 siblings born to Stormie and Mark Lynch.

    Her family is a music enthusiast. Her 3 brothers Riker, Rocky, and Ross are musicians, while their youngest brother Ryland Lynch was their manager while they were members of the pop rock band R.

    The siblings performed alongside their friend Ellington Ratliff. In 2018, the group changed its name across social media platforms to The Driver Era. Also, all posts R5 were deleted.

    It was later revealed that the band would consist of only Rocky and Ross. Nonetheless, Ellington, Rydle, and Riker still appear in many performances and concerts of the duo brother band.

    As a member of R5, she released famous albums such as Louder (2013) and Sometime Last Night (2015).

    Away from her immediate family, she is also cousins with famous people Derek and Julianne Hough. Both are singers, actors, and dancers.

    Apart From Music, She Started a Career in Acting in 2008

    Rydel Lynch made her acting debut in 2008 as a Crossroads Choir Member in Sunday School Musical. Although her acting career has not been as far as her music career, she has appeared in a number of American and Argentine TV series.

    They include So You Think You Can Dance (2009), A Day as Holly’s Kids (2011), Bunheads (2012), and Violetta (2015).

    She also appeared in an R5 movie All Day, All Night in 2015.

    She Has a Clothing Store and a YouTube Channel

    Music may seem at the forefront of Rydel’s career but she is also a YouTuber and a businesswoman. She has a channel called RydelFunk where she shares a lot about different topics, including makeup, fashion, food, family, and funny things among others.

    The channel was created in 2016 and she has over 509 thousand subscribers at the time of this report.

    As a businesswoman, she is the founder of an online boutique called Rydel (shoprydel.com). The store offers clothes of different types, including vintage.
